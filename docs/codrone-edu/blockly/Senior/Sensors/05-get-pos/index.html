get_pos()

Block#


Description#

Gets the current position of the CoDrone EDU.
The 'x' position of the drone is forwards and reverse.
The 'y' position of the drone is left and right.
The 'z' position of the drone is up and down.


Parameters#


type: x, y, z
unit: mm, cm, in, m

Returns#

Position value
